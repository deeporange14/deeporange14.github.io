Deep Orange 14 - Data Hub

Pioneering Skid-steered High-Speed Off-Road Autonomy

Welcome to the Deep Orange 14 (DO-14) Data Hub!

The DO-14 vehicle is Clemson University’s cutting-edge, skid-steered, tracked vehicle, created in association the US Army’s DEVCOM GVSC to revolutionize high-speed, off-road autonomy.

Dive into a wealth of data from DO-14’s extensive testing!

This portal provides datasets, design insights, and specs crafted to push the boundaries in vehicle autonomy, control, and safety. Whether you’re researching or engineering the future of autonomous ground vehicles, explore our resources to discover innovative solutions and valuable validation data in the dynamic world of high-speed, off-road autonomy.
